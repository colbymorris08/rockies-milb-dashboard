HOW THIS SHAPED THE MODEL

Extension weight (α=1.18) Highest α boost Top 20 averaged 6.7 ft extension. At altitude, every extra foot amplifies perceived velo more than at sea level — the model learned this from Freeland, Alcantara, and Skenes-era data.
Sinker penalty (α=0.79 IVB + 0.82 HB) Zero sinker-primary starters No top-20 pitcher relied on a sinker as their primary pitch. The model penalizes both IVB and Magnus HB simultaneously — doubling the altitude hit.
Gyro slider retention (α=0.97) 16 of 20 use gyro slider Gravity-driven horizontal break survives altitude nearly intact. The model's near-neutral gyro α is directly informed by slider-heavy pitchers outperforming here.
VAA boost (α=1.14) Avg VAA −5.1° Steeper entry angles disrupt timing when movement is suppressed. Top performers attack at steeper angles — and the model up-weights VAA accordingly.
